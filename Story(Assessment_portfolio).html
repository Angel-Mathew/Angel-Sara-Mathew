The summary of Lost and Found:The mother left to collect food leaving the chick in the cavity of the tree. As she was flying, the rain began pouring down, she flew even farther, but inside her, an uneasy feeling appeared: a cry for help. Unable to go further, she returned that time she saw the place had flooded,
and the tree where the chick was staying was halfway filled, trapping the chick. Now she is in a dilemma: if she tries to pick the chick, it might slip and fall into the water; or if she lets the chick stay, the tree could break or the chick could starve.

The summary of Valient girl:A small family lived near a forest, and Bianca, a girl, went to pick mangoes. She encountered a tiger and was chased inside her house. She heard a crying sound and found a girl, Ariel, who had been kidnapped from an orphanage. They found a table to climb out of the high window, and Ariel beat the man. They climbed out and went to Bianca's parents' house. The police arrested the man, who was later adopted by the parents.

The summary of Poppy:Olivia, a girl, found an injured rabbit named Poppy while walking to the stream. She named him after her rabbit and kept him on her bed. When her sister Arya heard a scream, Olivia told her parents, who were angry and threw the rabbit out. After a month, Olivia found Poppy thumping on his hind legs near her father, indicating something was wrong with him. She called her mother, who found him unconscious and took him to the hospital. The doctor told Olivia that her husband was doing better and had a heart attack. The mother allowed Olivia to keep Poppy, and Olivia thanked her mother for her help.

The summary of marvellous life of a parrot:Zale, a parrot from the Amazon forest, survives a forest fire and is captured, ending up in a pet shop. Eventually, he is bought by a man named Bob, who trains him for theft. Together, they successfully steal jewelry and a phone but are eventually caught due to a security camera. Bob is sent to jail, leaving Zale alone and saddened. Despite waiting for Bob's return, Zale eventually moves on, finding love and starting a family. Years later, Zale encounters Bob again, who has transformed his life positively after being released from jail. Bob shows remorse and kindness, offering nuts to Zale and his wife. This gesture symbolizes their enduring friendship and brings Zale great happiness. They happily reunite briefly before flying back to their homeland together.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------


The summary of The change of the mother earth:Alvin, on a mission to cut down a tree, is warned by the spirit of the tree not to proceed.
Terrified, he retreats but returns with friends, only to encounter another unsettling voice.
After a fall, Alvin is guided by a mysterious man who shows him a future Earth devastated
by pollution and industrialization. Terrified, Alvin is then taken to a fresh past where nature thrives.
Inspired to make a change, he vows to improve the present. With newfound determination, Alvin spreads awareness and initiates efforts to protect the environment worldwide.

The summary of Marsian:Resmi, a curious high school girl, encounters Martians who ask for water. She helps them, receives a blue rock, and keeps their visit a secret. Later, they return to thank her before leaving, leaving Resmi with a bittersweet memory.
